How DreamTube works
Turn a dream into a short AI-generated video — here's exactly how, and what it costs.
The idea
1
Type or record a short description of a dream you had.
2
Optionally add details: characters (including yourself, via a photo or a description), a camera angle, and the time/place the dream is set.
3
DreamTube generates a short video of your dream, using Google's Veo AI video model.
4
Keep it private, or share it to DreamTube's public Explore feed.
Pricing
Free, to start
Every account gets 200 tokens free on signup, plus 100 more free every 24 hours — no card required, ever, for the free allowance. Each video (a new one, an edit, or a regenerate) costs 100 tokens. That's enough for continued casual use at no cost.
